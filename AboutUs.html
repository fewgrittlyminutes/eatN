Our Story

Born from the need to simplify campus dining for students at NSBM Green University

The Beginning

Eat@N was born from a simple observation: students at NSBM Green University were spending valuable time searching for food options across campus. We realized there was a need for a centralized platform that could connect students with all available dining options.

What started as a student project has evolved into the premier food discovery platform for our campus community, serving over 1000+ students daily.

Our Mission

To revolutionize campus dining by providing students with seamless access to food options, real-time availability, and exceptional convenience. We strive to support both students and local food vendors through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading campus food platform in Sri Lanka, creating a connected community where students can easily discover, order, and enjoy quality meals while fostering relationships with local food entrepreneurs.
